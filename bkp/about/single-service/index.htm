    PLEASE RESPECTA Few of Our Simple Rules

    We have a few store rules to keep all our visiting families safe. Nothing fancy, just a few simple guidelines to ensure maximum fun with every visit to the playground.

    No outside food & drinks

    Outside food and drink is not permitted in store. Our on-site cafes serve great food and drinks all day.

    Children must wear socks

    For safety and hygiene reasons all playing children and supervising adults must wear socks in our play areas.

    Parent supervision

    Active supervision of your children is required at all times, staff are on hand to provide assistance in an emergency.
